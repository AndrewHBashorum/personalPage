About Me

I graduated from South East Technical University with a bachelor's degree with Honours in Software Development. I spent my first year in college studying Games Development. This gave me a love of difficult problem-solving and coding but seeing few job opportunities in Game Development I switched courses to software in my second year. I started working with Lanu towards the end of my second year.

Lanu is a London-based prop-tech startup that has ambitions of codifying the planning rules. This meant giving rapid feedback on the legal and possible extensions a homeowner could recieve.

I worked for Lanu part-time, as an intern, and full-time throughout different periods for a total of about 2+ years. During this time I was mainly responsible for data collection, cleaning, and storage but I also gained knowledge of Amazon web services, Docker, and Postgres Databases. I also helped to maintain and refactor a PyQGIS internal application and a ruby-based web application.
